My First Palette With Adobe Creative Cloud Capture Inspired By Kehinde Wiley

This image by Kehinde Wiley inspired the color palette used for The Elementary MBA logo. Below you'll see the first draft of the logo. I was introduced to Kehinde Wiley's work by a friend, Andia Winslow, during a trip to the Phoenix Museum of Contemporary Art. A few years later another friend, Annabel Wong, mentioned him and his work to me. Both of these individuals had the opportunity to meet Kehinde during their travels on the East Coast.

By the way, the Phx Museum of Contemporary Art is FREE on Wednesdays and it's a great place to go on dates, especially first dates.
